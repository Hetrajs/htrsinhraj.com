About Us

Benzo-Chem Industries

BENZOCHEM INDS has carved niche for itself as a well-reckoned Manufacturer and Exporter of top quality Industrial Chemicals. We are engaged in making available accurately formulated Industrial Chemicals such as Butyl Tiglate, Ethyl Tiglate, General Tiglate, Methyl Tiglate, Tiglic Acid, Malonic Acid and Heptanone Acid. The Industrial Chemicals are known for their wide industrial applications. Thus, the Industrial Chemicals are demanded in diverse industries. Clients can avail Industrial Chemicals from us in leak proof packing. The foundation of BENZOCHEM INDS was laid in Ankleshwar (Gujarat). The company is running very smoothly with the capable guidance of MR. JASHVANTSINH RAJ. He has taken the company to all new levels of success.
